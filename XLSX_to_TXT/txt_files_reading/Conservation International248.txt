 in 2008, ci re-imagined conservation.org to make ci’s work more accessible to the millions who gather information on the web. the revamped web site serves as the gateway for all ci programs and regions. innovations include the online “stop the clock on species extinctions” campaign, expanded video content (featuring ci partners and supporters, including al gore and pearl jam), a personal carbon calculator and the launch of a climate change campaign featuring a harrison ford public service announcement that appeared worldwide.
