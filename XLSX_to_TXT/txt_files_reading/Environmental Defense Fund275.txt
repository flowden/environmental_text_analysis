 sharks have ruled the oceans for millions of years. today, they’re in serious decline. working with mote marine laboratory and others, edf is leading an effort to save sharks in the gulf of mexico— bringing together cuba, m exico and the united states in an unprecedented conservation partnership. we are conducting the first-ever in-depth survey of sharks along cuba’s northwest coast and mexico’s gulf coast. the research will reveal the special places that warrant protection and could be a model for managing other threatened migratory species such as tuna.
