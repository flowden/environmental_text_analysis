 can protecting nature alleviate poverty? in january 2012, researchers from ci, led by dr. will turner, published a study that indicates a strong connection worldwide. the research shows that, every year, nature provides services like pollination and clean water that together have an estimated value of more than $1 trillion to poor communities. the key—and central to ci’s work—is sustaining these critical natural ecosystems and ensuring that people have access to the benefits they provide.
