 the waters of south florida harbor the continental united states’ largest coral reef. the conservancy convened a group of scientists, resource managers, coral reef stakeholders and other conservation groups to develop a toolbox of resiliency-based management actions for coral reef managers. a major focus for the coming years will be to develop agreement for an integrated resilient reef management plan of florida’s reefs from the st. lucie inlet to the dry tortugas. a florida project to restore the federally threatened staghorn coral is underway at coral nursery sites in the dry tortugas, the florida keys and biscayne bay national park.
