 in 2018, wwf partnered with international paper to restore 250 high-priority acres in brazil’s species-rich atlantic forest, home to the iconic jaguar. with the first round of companysupported planting underway, this year wwf also teamed up with hp to restore more than 1,200 acres of this native forest. in addition, hp’s support will enable wwf and partners to improve management of more than 197,000 acres in china while increasing consumer awareness of—and demand for—responsibly sourced forest products.
