 a coalition of caribbean island nations announced bold commitments to conserve at least 20% of their marine and coastal habitats by 2020, and the nature conservancy pledged an initial $20 million in support. the conservancy has been instrumental in developing these “island challenge” initiatives— including the micronesia challenge and coral triangle initiative—whereby island governments with limited internal resources agree to dramatically expand conservation with investment from development agencies and governments. the conservancy’s new european office in berlin helped broker financing from german agencies.
