 our mission is to promote efforts to educate and empower people to protect and improve the natural and human environment. the sierra club foundation bridges the intersection of strategic philanthropy and grassroots advocacy. founded in 1960, we are a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt public charity governed by an independent board of directors. we achieve our shared vision of a healthy planet through collaboration with the sierra club, our donors, and stakeholders. we embrace integrity through ethics, excellence, and transparency to ensure the trust of our donors and partners. we demonstrate stewardship in all we do through careful development and management of resources and responsible oversight of sponsored programs.
