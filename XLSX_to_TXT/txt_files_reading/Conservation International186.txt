 the critical ecosystem partnership fund (cepf) — a joint initiative of conservation international and five other partners — achieved multiple impacts around the world in the past year. in september 2017, cepf completed its first five-year investment in the mediterranean basin — a crucial and oftenoverlooked hotspot of biodiversity — awarding 108 grants to 93 organizations for a total investment of $10.7 million across 11 countries. cepf has awarded $232 million in grants since its inception in 2000. grantees working in 93 countries and territories have helped establish 14.8 million hectares of protected area, benefiting 1,250 globally threatened species and more than 3,000 communities.
