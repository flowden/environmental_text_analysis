 goal: conserve wildlife by helping landowners protect habitat. why: the unprecedented pace of energy development in the west has put the industry on a collision course with wildlife. our objectives by 2015: 1/ a habitat exchange for the lesser prairie chicken has been created and approved by state and federal regulators. 2 / three new habitat exchanges launch in target states affected by energy development. 3/ half a million acres of prairie chicken and sage grouse habitat are conserved or restored.
