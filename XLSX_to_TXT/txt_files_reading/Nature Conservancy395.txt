 california’s offshore banks, kelp beds and underwater canyons sustain a dazzling array of species, including seabirds, seals, dolphins, whales and tuna. in a recent fishing season, west coast trawlers landed 50 million pounds of target groundfish and discarded 46.6 million pounds of “by-catch.” the trawler-buyout program is one innovative strategy among many that the conservancy’s global marine initiative uses to benefit marine life and local economies.
