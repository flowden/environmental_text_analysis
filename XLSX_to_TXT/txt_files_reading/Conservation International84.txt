 in fy14, ci and birdlife international led a coalition of seven european organizations that successfully championed a us$ 800 million increase in funding for international conservation in the european union’s 2014- 2020 budget—bringing the total investment to $2 billion. in the u.s., over $10 billion was secured in fy11-fy14 congressional appropriations for development assistance and the global environment facility, providing crucial funding for forest conservation, wildlife protection, healthy marine ecosystems, sustainable landscapes and adaptation.
