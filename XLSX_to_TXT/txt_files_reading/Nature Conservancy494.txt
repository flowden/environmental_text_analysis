 perhaps the best way to combat environmental degradation is to stop it before it starts. that’s the idea behind the community visioning plan spearheaded by the nature conservancy in pohnpei’s municipality of u. completed through the collaboration of 67 government, community and religious leaders, the plan outlines a 20-year strategy to protect u’s fragile habitats while promoting economic development. this is the first plan of its kind in the federated states of micronesia.
