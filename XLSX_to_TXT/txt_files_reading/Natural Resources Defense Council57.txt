 nrdc's eighth farm to table dinner benefit took place at williamsburg's north brooklyn f'arms, where more than 100 guests enjoyed an evening of cocktails, bluegrass music, a farm tour, and a dinner catered by the popular neighborhood restaurant marlow & sons. the event-attended by nrdc trustees, major donors, new york council members, and e2 members-also included remarks from nrdc chief counsel mitch bernard and senior attorneys margaret brown and jen grossman. nrdc's experts discussed their efforts to create transformative change locally as they work to advance a sustainable, healthy, and equitable food system.
