 in october 2007  international director general jim leape joined nobel laureates climate experts and politians led by german chancellor angela merkel at an international symposium in potsdam germany to discuss ways to boost environmental sustainability and curb damaging climate change he chaired the session on agendas  polies for the future stressing the importance of carbon markets as one of the most effective instruments in tackling climate change “giving carbon a price is one of the major challenges in the future and  is ready to make a significant contribution to creating and framing this emerging market”
