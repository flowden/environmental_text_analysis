 indigenous peoples and local communities steward nearly a quarter of the earth’s land and the vast majority of its wildlife — and studies have shown that local management can result in better conservation outcomes and more stable livelihoods recognizing the role of these communities  helped found the critical ecosystem partnership fund in 2000 the fund empowers local people working on some of the world’s most pressing conservation issues from restoring wildlife on pafic islands to supporting sustainable ranching in the rangelands of africa to date the fund has improved the sustainability of 41 million hectares 101 million acres of land and water — an area larger than germany — helping 2600 communities worldwide
