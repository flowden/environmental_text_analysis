 the us department of defense dod has been a longterm partner in efforts to conserve and enhance the longleaf pine forests of the southeast some of the largest and healthiest stands of longleaf occur on military bases whose readiness depends in part on the health and resilience of those forests in 2020 we worked with dod to launch another conservation program this one focused on the health of forests and watersheds in guam a strategically important and ecologically rich us territory
