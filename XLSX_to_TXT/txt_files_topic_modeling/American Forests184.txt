 the big trees program was introduced in january 1940 with an article in  “wanted’’ it read the location and measurement of the largest spemens of the following american tree spees’ the list that followed contained 100 spees and by 1945 when the first compilation was published 218 trees were listed as national champions the list has since grown to over 650 1984 was a busy year for big trees the national register of big trees was updated in  local and national publications carried stories on the program several dedicated volunteers assisted in reorganizing the 44year collection of big tree files and state coordinators continued to volunteer their services to provide the backbone of the program
