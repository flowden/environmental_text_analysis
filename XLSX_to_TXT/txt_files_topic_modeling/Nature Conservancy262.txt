 mexico’s new ocampo flora and fauna protected area is the final piece of the puzzle for one of the largest binational conservation regions in the world uniting two mexican protected areas with big bend national park big bend ranch state park and the conservancy’s davis mountains preserve across the rio grande in texas the conservancy and partners spent five years completing a justification study and management plan and garnering local landowner support for ocampo the conservancy has also been promoting ecological restoration and ecofriendly enterprises in the region
