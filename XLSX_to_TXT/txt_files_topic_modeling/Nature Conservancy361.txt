 dr alison green recently led the design of the world’s first marine protected area network aimed at minimizing climate change impacts on coral reefs in papua new guinea—a model that is being used around the world key to the design is reef resilience a revolutionary concept devel oped by the conservancy and partners that involves identifying and protect ing areas where coral reefs are most likely to resist bleaching and other damage larvae from the resilient corals can then repopulate reefs destroyed by bleaching it is one of the many strategies the conservancy is pursuing both on land and in the water to help plants animals natural areas and people adapt to unavoidable effects of climate change
