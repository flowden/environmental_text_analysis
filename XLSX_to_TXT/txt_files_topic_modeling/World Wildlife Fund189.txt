 after years of sentific research advocacy and community and government engagement by cambodia and our partners the government of cambodia abandoned plans to build the sambor hydropower dam on the mekong river and put a 10year halt on future dam construction on the river’s main artery a freeflowing mekong protects the world’s most productive freshwater fishery and supports breathtaking biodiversity including the largest population of irrawaddy river dolphins on earth cambodia is poised to support federal development of a sustainable energy plan that promotes clean and renewable energy alternatives while keeping the mighty mekong intact
